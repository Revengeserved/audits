# Recovery Scoring System

## Purpose

The recovery score ranks evidence and leads by how likely they are to help recover or identify crypto-related assets.

## Scores

- **95–100:** Complete recovery artifact or direct access material. Requires strict private handling and must not be committed to GitHub.
- **80–94:** Strong evidence of wallet recovery material. Requires manual review.
- **60–79:** Likely wallet-related lead. Needs investigation.
- **40–59:** Possibly relevant but lower confidence.
- **0–39:** Unlikely wallet-related.

## Evidence Standard

Every score must include the reason it was assigned. Do not invent facts, do not fill gaps, and do not assume ownership.

## Recommended Handling

- Store only redacted references in GitHub.
- Keep sensitive artifacts out of the repository.
- Use local encrypted storage for private material.
- Prefer public blockchain data, exported CSVs, and screenshots with sensitive fields removed.
